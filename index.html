Peanuts' Days Left in School Game

Game Rules

Everyone in the Peanuts gang, even Snoopy and Woodstock can't wait for school to be over.

Help the gang to get out of school by reducing their number of days left in school

Kids and Adults can play along. Kids get less characters and less less.

Click on a Peanuts character to subtract a specific amount of days from your total days left in school.

Pay attention. Each character is worth a different number of days

You win the game by getting the days left in School to Zero!

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each Peanuts character.
