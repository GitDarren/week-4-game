Peanuts' Days Left in School Game

Game Rules

Everyone in the Peanuts gang, even Snoopy and Woodstock can't wait for school to be over.

You will be given a random number of days at the start of the game.

There are four Peanuts characters to choose from. By clicking on a Peanuts character you will subtract a specific amount of days from your total days left in school.

You win the game by getting the days left in School to Zero!

The value of Peanuts character is hidden from you until you click on them.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each Peanuts character.
